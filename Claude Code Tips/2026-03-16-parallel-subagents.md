> [!tweet] RileyRalmuto · Mar 16, 2026
> here's a claude code prompt series that can change your life:
> 
> "hey friend, will you spin up 3 to 5 parallel subagents to research [concept/idea] from every angle - what exists, what's been tried, what the best implementations look like, and what we can learn from adjacent projects. write their findings to /research.
> 
> don't just summarize what you find - treat every finding as raw inspiration. after researching, step back and think deeply about what's missing from everything that exists, what patterns cut across the best implementations, and what nobody has tried yet. then write an opinionated, creative proposal for how we should build [thing] - not a safe composite of what's out there, but something that takes the best ideas further than anyone has."
> 
> then
> 
> "now take everything from round one as your current best understanding. Spawn a second round of parallel subagents - but this time, each agent's job is to challenge and surpass what we already have. search specifically for things that contradict our assumptions, outperform our favorite implementations, or solve problems we didn't even identifyy yet. compare everything you find directly against our round one findings - what's better, what's worse, what changes our thinking? update the proposal only where the new findings genuinely improve it, and explain what changed and why."
