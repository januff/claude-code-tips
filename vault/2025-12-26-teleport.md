## Summary

The `claude --teleport` command enables seamless session handoff between devices. Execute the command in your terminal, specifying the session ID, to synchronize your desktop Claude session with your active web or mobile session. This allows you to pick up coding tasks on your PC exactly where you left off on another device, ensuring a consistent workflow across platforms.
